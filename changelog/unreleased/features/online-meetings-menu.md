**Online Meetings Menu**: New dedicated view for online meetings
- Added "Online Meetings" menu item in the sidebar navigation
- Displays all online meetings in a vertical list grouped by day of the week
- Days are ordered starting from today (current day first)
- Auto-scrolls to current time on page load
- Shows "Now" marker between past and upcoming meetings
- Floating "Back to Now" button appears when scrolled away from current time
- Direct "Join" button for meetings with online URLs
- Meeting detail sidebar for viewing full meeting information
